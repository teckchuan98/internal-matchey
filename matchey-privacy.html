This Privacy Policy explains what data Matchey collects, how that data is used, when it is shared, and how users are informed before AI-related data is transmitted to a third-party provider.

1. Information We Collect

We collect account and profile details, messages and media users choose to submit, selfie and verification content, and technical/service diagnostics needed to operate the app.

2. How We Collect Information

Information is collected directly from users during signup, onboarding, messaging, reporting, photo uploads, and verification flows, and indirectly through app/server diagnostics needed to keep the service running.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to provide messaging, profile display, account security, moderation, abuse prevention, verification, and customer support.

4. Third-Party AI Use

When users explicitly invoke AI features such as @momo or AI-supported verification, relevant data may be sent to OpenAI, L.L.C. only after the app presents a dedicated AI consent notice. The notice identifies the recipient, the categories of data sent, and the purpose of the processing.

5. Sharing

We may share data with service providers that support authentication, storage, notifications, and AI-powered features. We require providers handling personal data on our behalf to maintain appropriate security and privacy protections.

Questions regarding this policy can be sent to codeboi98@gmail.com.
